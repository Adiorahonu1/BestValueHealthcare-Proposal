01: Overview

Executive Summary

Best Value Healthcare Services currently manages aide onboarding and shift scheduling through manual or fragmented processes. This proposal outlines a modern digital platform that streamlines the full aide lifecycle, from application and document verification, through admin approval, to mobile-based shift management.

🌐
Web Onboarding Portal

Aides register online, upload all required compliance documents, and track their application status in real time. Admins review, approve, or reject applicants from a centralized dashboard.

📱
Mobile Shift Management App

Once approved, aides download the app and immediately access the shift board, browsing open client shifts, claiming with one tap, dropping when needed, and receiving push notifications for new opportunities.

03: Features

Platform Feature Breakdown

Four integrated modules, all connected to your CRM, working together as one cohesive system.

🌐 Web Onboarding Portal
  • Aide registration form, name, contact, credentials, experience
  • Multi-document upload with drag & drop + mobile camera capture
  • Real-time status tracker: Pending → Under Review → Approved / Rejected
  • Email & SMS notification on every status change
  • Secure, encrypted document storage (AWS S3)
  • New aide records automatically created in your CRM on approval
🖥️ Admin Dashboard (Web)
  • View all applicant submissions in one place
  • Review uploaded documents in-browser, no downloads needed
  • One-click approve or reject with optional notes to applicant
  • Bulk actions across multiple applicants
  • Full audit log of every admin action
  • Approval status synced to CRM in real time, no manual data entry
📱 Mobile App: Shift Management (iOS & Android)
  • Approved-only login, tied directly to application status
  • Claim a shift with a single tap, instant confirmation
  • Shift board: browse by date, time, location, client name
  • Drop a shift (configurable notice window, TBD)
  • Push notifications: new shifts, reminders, approval status
  • In-app profile viewer & document wallet
  • Shift history log, full record of past and upcoming shifts
  • Real-time updates when new shifts are posted
  • Shift completions logged to CRM automatically
🔗 CRM Integration: Everything Connected
  • Every approved aide is pushed to your CRM as a contact automatically
  • Application status updates sync in real time, no double entry
  • Shifts claimed and dropped are logged against the aide's CRM record
  • Document expiration dates tracked and flagged inside your CRM
  • Trigger CRM automations on approval, welcome sequences, onboarding tasks
  • Shift history visible per aide directly from their CRM profile
  • Two-way sync, changes in your CRM reflect in the platform
  • Compatible with major CRMs (GoHighLevel, HubSpot, Salesforce, and more)
